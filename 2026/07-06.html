Unki Yaadein

Humare pyaare dada ji hume chhodkar chale gaye. Unka jeevan hum sabhi ke liye ek prerna srot tha. Unki kahi baatein, unka muskurata chehra aur unka sikhaya rasta humesha humare sath rahega. Hum unhe har pal yaad karenge.

"Unki sikhayi hui koi pyari baat ya unka sabse pasandida vachan yahan likhein."
