Zindagi Ka Ek Naya Adhyay

Zindagi hamesha ek jaisi nahi rehti. Yeh humein har mod par kuch naya sikhati hai—kabhi khushi ke pal deti hai, toh kabhi aise mushkil daur se guzarti hai jahan humein andar se majboot banna padta hai.

Waqt chahe jaisa bhi ho, bitaye gaye pal aur apno ki yaadein hamesha hamare sath rehti hain. Unhi yaadon ke sahare humein aage badhna hota hai aur un sapno ko poora karna hota hai jo unhone hamare liye dekhe the.
